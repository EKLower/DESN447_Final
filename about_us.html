About Us

Hello we are Jackson's Bakery!

My name is Minnie Jackson and I started Jackson's Bakery several years ago. I have always loved to bake and wanted to share my creations with more than just my family. For a few years it was just the bakery, but I wanted to be able to offer my yummy treats to even more people. With my son going off to college, this bakery has provided me with the resources to be able to help him. Thank you to all of our customers, we never could have done it without you.
